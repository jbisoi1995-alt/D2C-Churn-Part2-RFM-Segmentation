# Retention Strategy Recommendations

## Champions

Characteristics:

* Highest spending customers
* Most frequent purchases
* Lowest churn rate

Actions:

* VIP loyalty rewards
* Early access to new products
* Exclusive offers and memberships

Priority: Medium

---

## Loyal Customers

Characteristics:

* Consistent purchase behavior
* Strong engagement

Actions:

* Personalized recommendations
* Cross-sell and upsell campaigns
* Loyalty point incentives

Priority: High

---

## Potential Loyalists

Characteristics:

* Recently active customers
* Moderate spending levels

Actions:

* Welcome journeys
* Personalized promotions
* Product discovery campaigns

Priority: High

---

## At Risk Customers

Characteristics:

* Increasing recency
* Declining engagement

Actions:

* Win-back campaigns
* Targeted discounts
* Personalized communication

Priority: Very High

---

## Lost Customers

Characteristics:

* Long inactivity period
* Highest churn probability

Actions:

* Re-engagement campaigns
* Strong promotional offers
* Feedback surveys to identify reasons for churn

Priority: Critical

---

## Business Recommendation

The company should focus retention investments primarily on At Risk and Loyal Customers because these groups offer the best balance between revenue potential and recovery probability. Lost Customers should receive low-cost automated reactivation campaigns, while Champions should be protected through premium loyalty programs.
